Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

SLAMA Consulting ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SportTracking mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our app. By using SportTracking, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)

1.2 Physical and Health Data

To provide fitness tracking features, we collect:

  • Weight and height
  • Fitness level
  • Calories burned (calculated from your activity and profile data)

1.3 Location Data

During active sport tracking sessions, we collect precise GPS data including:

  • Latitude and longitude coordinates
  • Altitude
  • Speed
  • GPS accuracy
  • Timestamps for each recorded point

Location data is only collected when you actively start a tracking session. We do not track your location in the background.

1.4 Activity Data

  • Sport type selected
  • Distance traveled
  • Duration of sessions
  • Average and maximum speed
  • Pace and elevation changes
  • Weather conditions at the time of your activity (temperature, weather code)

1.5 User-Generated Content

  • Chat messages within event groups
  • Event descriptions and titles
  • Photos uploaded for profile, events, or chat

1.6 Device and Technical Data

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token for push notifications
  • Device model and operating system version (via crash reporting)
  • App performance metrics (startup time, network latency)

1.7 Usage Data

  • Screen views and navigation patterns
  • Feature usage and interactions (e.g., button taps, sport selections)
  • Crash logs and error reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core features: Track your sport activities, calculate performance metrics, and display your history
  • Enable social features: Allow you to participate in community events, chat with other users, and appear on leaderboards
  • Personalize your experience: Calculate calories based on your physical profile, suggest relevant sports, and customize your dashboard
  • Send notifications: Inform you about event invitations, chat messages, and activity updates
  • Improve the app: Analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and optimize performance
  • Display advertisements: Show personalized ads through Google AdMob (with your consent where required by law)
  • Ensure safety: Detect cheating in sport tracking, moderate chat content, and enforce community guidelines

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

3.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user accounts and authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores user data, trips, events, and messages
  • Firebase Storage: Stores uploaded photos
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Delivers push notifications
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports for debugging
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring: Monitors app performance

For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

3.2 Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (Advertising ID), interaction data, and IP addresses. Ad personalization is subject to your GDPR consent choices.

For more information, see Google Privacy Policy.

3.3 Google Maps

We use Google Maps SDK to display your GPS tracks and event locations on maps. Google Maps may collect usage data according to its own privacy policy.

3.4 Open-Meteo

We use the Open-Meteo API to fetch weather information during your sport sessions. Your GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) are sent to Open-Meteo to retrieve local weather data. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather API.

3.5 Google Play Billing

In-app purchases (premium subscription) are processed through Google Play Billing. Payment information is handled entirely by Google Play.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Firebase cloud services (hosted by Google) and locally on your device using encrypted storage. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Firebase Authentication secures user accounts
  • Firestore Security Rules restrict data access
  • Local database is excluded from Android backup and device transfer
  • Screen capture is blocked in release builds

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account, your data is soft-deleted and may be permanently removed within 30 days
  • Trip data: GPS points and trip records are retained until you manually delete them or delete your account
  • Chat messages: Messages are retained within their associated events
  • Crash and analytics data: Retained according to Firebase default retention policies (typically 90 days for crash data)

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update your personal information through the app's profile editor
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data from within the app
  • Consent withdrawal: Modify your advertising consent at any time through the app's Privacy Settings
  • Data portability: Export your trip data as PDF from the app

To exercise these rights, use the in-app features or contact us at the address below.

7. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain consent for personalized advertising. You can manage your consent preferences at any time through the "Privacy Settings" option in your profile.

8. CCPA/CPRA Compliance (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources of collection, the business purposes, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. You can delete your account directly within the app through the Profile settings.
  • Right to Correct: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information. You can update your profile information directly in the app.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell your personal information in the traditional sense. However, our use of Google AdMob for personalized advertising may constitute "sharing" under the CCPA/CPRA. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through the "Privacy Settings" option in your profile.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA/CPRA rights.

To exercise these rights, use the in-app features or contact us at slama.walid1985@gmail.com. We will respond to verifiable requests within 45 days.

9. Children's Privacy

SportTracking is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

10. AI Coach & Training Plans

SportTracking includes an AI-powered coaching feature that provides personalized sport coaching and training plans. The AI Coach uses your profile data (fitness level, weight, height), activity history, and goals to generate tailored recommendations.

  • AI Coach conversations and generated training plans are stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Your conversation history is used to provide contextual coaching advice
  • AI responses are powered by Google Gemini and are for informational purposes only — they do not constitute professional medical or fitness advice
  • Daily usage limits apply to AI Coach interactions
  • You can delete individual conversations or all AI Coach data at any time from the app

11. Rewarded Video Ads

Free users can watch rewarded video advertisements to temporarily unlock certain premium features (limited to 5 per day). When you choose to watch a rewarded ad:

  • Ad viewing events are tracked locally to deliver the correct reward and enforce daily limits
  • No additional personal data is collected beyond what Google AdMob already processes
  • Rewarded ad access resets when the app is restarted
  • Rewarded ads are subject to Google AdMob policies and your GDPR consent preferences

12. Referral Program

SportTracking offers a referral program where users can invite others using unique referral codes:

  • A unique referral code is generated for each user and stored in Firestore
  • Referral data (code, referrer ID, referee ID, redemption status) is stored to track and deliver rewards
  • Both the referrer and new user receive a 7-day Premium trial upon successful code redemption
  • No personal information is shared between the referrer and the referred user
  • Abuse of the referral system (e.g., creating multiple accounts) may result in account suspension

13. Event Reminders

SportTracking sends reminder notifications 3 hours before your upcoming events:

  • Reminders are processed locally on your device using Android WorkManager — no server calls are made
  • Reminder preferences and tracking data (which events have been reminded) are stored locally in DataStore
  • Reminders appear both as system notifications and in the in-app Notifications page
  • You can disable event reminders in the app Settings under Notifications
  • Reminder data is automatically cleaned up when events expire

14. Chat Moderation

To ensure a safe community experience, we implement the following moderation features:

  • Users can report inappropriate messages with a reason
  • Users can block other users to hide their messages
  • Automated moderation: users who receive multiple reports may have their messages removed and be muted in events
  • Severely reported accounts may be suspended

Report data (including the reported message text and reason) is stored to enable moderation. Users can delete their own messages at any time.

15. Sport Story

The Sport Story feature uses Google Gemini AI to generate a narrative of your athletic journey based on your trip history, performance data, and fitness level. Generated stories are cached locally and in Firestore. Shared story images contain your performance statistics. Daily generation limits apply. This feature is available to Premium subscribers or by watching a rewarded ad.

They do not constitute professional medical or fitness advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting or modifying any exercise program. SLAMA Consulting is not liable for any injury, health issue, or adverse outcome resulting from the use of these estimates or recommendations.
