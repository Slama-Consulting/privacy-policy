Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

SLAMA Consulting ("we", "our", or "us") operates the QuranQuest mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our app. By using QuranQuest, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo (optional, via Google Sign-In or manual upload)
  • Preferred language (Arabic, French, or English)

1.2 Quran Memorization Progress

To provide Hifz (memorization) tracking features, we collect:

  • Surah and ayah progress (which verses you are studying)
  • Mastery level for each verse (not started, discovered, learning, memorized, mastered, cemented)
  • Review schedule data (spaced repetition intervals, ease factor, repetition count)
  • Session records (date, duration, score, verses covered)

1.3 Reading Progress

  • Last surah and ayah read
  • List of completed surahs
  • Khatma count (number of complete Quran readings)
  • Last read timestamp

1.4 Gamification Data

  • Experience points (XP) and level/rank
  • Activity streak (consecutive days of practice)
  • Badges earned and progress toward badges
  • Leaderboard ranking (weekly and all-time)
  • Hasanat (in-app game currency) balance

1.5 Audio Recordings

When you use the Tajweed recitation testing feature, your voice is recorded temporarily for analysis. Audio recordings are processed locally or sent to an AI service for analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers.

1.6 Device and Technical Data

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token for push notifications
  • Device model and operating system version (via crash reporting, if consented)

1.7 Usage Data (with your consent)

  • Screen views and navigation patterns
  • Feature usage and interactions
  • Crash logs and error reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core features: Track your Quran memorization and reading progress, schedule reviews using spaced repetition, and display your history
  • Enable gamification: Calculate XP, manage streaks and badges, and display leaderboard rankings
  • Sync across devices: Synchronize your progress between devices using your Firebase account
  • Send reminders: Deliver daily memorization reminders and notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve the app: Analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and optimize performance (only with your consent)
  • Display advertisements: Show ads through Google AdMob for free-tier users (premium subscribers see no ads)
  • Provide AI analysis: Analyze recitation quality for Tajweed testing (premium feature)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

3.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user accounts (email/password and Google Sign-In)
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores user profiles, memorization progress, gamification data, and leaderboard entries
  • Firebase Storage: Stores uploaded profile photos
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Delivers push notifications and reminders
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage statistics (opt-in only)
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports for debugging (opt-in only)

For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

3.2 Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob for free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers, interaction data, and IP addresses. Premium subscribers are not shown any advertisements.

For more information, see Google Privacy Policy.

3.3 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions (monthly and yearly premium plans). RevenueCat processes subscription status and purchase receipts. Payment information is handled entirely by Google Play.

For more information, see RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

3.4 Google Gemini AI

For premium users, recitation audio may be analyzed using Google Gemini AI for advanced Tajweed feedback. Audio data sent for analysis is not stored after processing.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Firebase cloud services (hosted by Google) and locally on your device. We implement security measures including:

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Firebase Authentication secures user accounts
  • Firestore Security Rules restrict data access to authorized users only
  • Local data is stored using Hive encrypted database and SharedPreferences
  • Sensitive credentials are stored using Flutter Secure Storage

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account, all your personal data and progress is permanently removed from our servers immediately
  • Memorization data: Hifz progress, sessions, and reading data are retained until you delete your account
  • Analytics data: Retained according to Firebase default retention policies (if consented)
  • Crash reports: Retained for up to 90 days (if consented)
  • Audio recordings: Temporary only; deleted immediately after Tajweed analysis

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update your personal information through the app's profile settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Settings > Delete Account)
  • Consent withdrawal: Modify your analytics and crash reporting consent at any time (Settings > Manage Consent)

To exercise these rights, use the in-app features or contact us at the address below.

7. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • A consent screen is displayed on first launch, allowing you to opt in or out of analytics and crash reporting
  • You can modify your consent preferences at any time from the app's Settings
  • You can delete all your data by deleting your account
  • Analytics and Crashlytics are disabled by default when consent is refused

8. Children's Privacy

QuranQuest includes a Child Mode feature designed for young users. When Child Mode is enabled:

  • External links (including privacy policy links) are blocked to prevent leaving the app
  • Analytics data collection is automatically disabled
  • Advertisements may still be shown but are not personalized
  • A parental PIN (4-digit code) is required to disable Child Mode

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13 without consent, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

9. Leaderboard and Public Data

If you participate in the leaderboard feature, the following information is publicly visible to other QuranQuest users:

  • Display name
  • Profile photo
  • Total XP and weekly XP
  • Rank and level
  • Badge count
  • Activity streak

This data is visible only within the app and is removed when you delete your account.

10. Offline Mode

QuranQuest stores data locally on your device to enable offline usage. This includes memorization progress, reading position, and cached leaderboard data. Local data is synced with our servers when an internet connection is available.

11. In-App Purchases

QuranQuest offers premium subscriptions (monthly at €4.99 and yearly at €35.99) through Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. All payment processing is handled by Google Play. Subscription management is facilitated by RevenueCat.

12. Android Permissions

QuranQuest requests the following device permissions:

  • Internet: Required for data synchronization and cloud features
  • Microphone: Required for Tajweed recitation testing (only used when you start a recitation session)
  • Notifications: Required for daily memorization reminders and push notifications
  • Alarm scheduling: Required for scheduling daily reminder notifications
  • Billing: Required for in-app subscription purchases

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
