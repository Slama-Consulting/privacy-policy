Delete Your Data

Last updated: March 10, 2026

QuranQuest by SLAMA Consulting allows you to manage and delete specific data without deleting your entire account. This page explains what data you can delete and how to do it.

1. Reset Memorization Progress (Hifz)

You can reset your memorization progress for specific surahs or all surahs.

1
Open Hifz (Memorization) Tap the Hifz tab in the bottom navigation bar.
2
Select a surah Tap on the surah whose progress you want to reset.
3
Reset progress Use the reset option to clear your memorization progress for that surah. This removes mastery levels, review schedules, and session history for the selected surah.

Data deleted: Mastery levels, spaced repetition data (intervals, ease factor, repetitions), review dates, and session scores for the selected surah. Deleted from both your device and our servers.

2. Manage Analytics and Crash Reporting Consent

You can withdraw your consent for analytics and crash reporting data collection at any time.

1
Open Settings Go to your Profile, then tap the settings icon (gear) in the top-right corner.
2
Tap "Manage Consent" In the Privacy section, tap "Manage consent".
3
Disable data collection Toggle off "Allow usage analytics" and/or "Allow crash reports", or tap "Reject all" to disable both.

Effect: Firebase Analytics and/or Firebase Crashlytics will stop collecting data immediately. Previously collected anonymous data may be retained by Firebase for up to 14 months (analytics) or 90 days (crash reports) but cannot be linked to your identity.

3. Reset App Settings

You can reset all app settings to their default values.

1
Open Settings Go to your Profile, then tap the settings icon (gear).
2
Tap "Reset settings" Scroll to the bottom and tap the "Reset settings" button.
3
Confirm A confirmation dialog will appear. Tap "Reset" to restore all settings to defaults.

Data reset: Theme mode, language, font size, Arabic font, notification preferences, and child mode settings are restored to defaults. Your account and progress data are not affected.

4. Clear Local Cached Data

QuranQuest stores data locally on your device for offline access. You can clear this local cache through your device settings:

1
Open Android Settings Go to your device's Settings > Apps > QuranQuest.
2
Clear Cache or Clear Data Tap "Clear Cache" to remove temporary files, or "Clear Data" to remove all local data including your login session.

Data cleared: Locally cached memorization progress, leaderboard data, downloaded audio files, and app preferences. Your data on our servers is not affected and will be re-synced when you log in again.

5. Data You Can Manage

Data Type How to Delete / Manage Where Deleted
Hifz memorization progress Reset per surah in the Hifz section Device + Server
Analytics consent Settings > Manage Consent Stops future collection
Crash reporting consent Settings > Manage Consent Stops future collection
App settings and preferences Settings > Reset Settings Device only
Local cached data Android Settings > Apps > QuranQuest > Clear Cache Device only
Profile information Edit your profile in the Profile tab Device + Server
Push notifications Disable in Settings > Notifications Stops future notifications

6. Delete Your Entire Account

If you want to delete your account and ALL associated data (memorization progress, gamification, leaderboard, badges, and personal information), please visit our Account Deletion page for instructions.
