Delete Your Account

Last updated: March 13, 2026

SLAMA Consulting ("we", "our", or "us") respects your right to delete your account and associated data from the SportTracking mobile application. This page explains how to request account deletion and what data is affected.

⚠ Warning: This action is permanent

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your personal data, trip history, event participation, chat messages, challenge progress, leaderboard scores, AI Coach conversations, training plans, referral data, and saved routes. This action cannot be undone.

1. How to Delete Your Account

Option A: Delete from within the app (Recommended)

1
Open SportTracking Launch the app and make sure you are logged in.
2
Go to your Profile Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar.
3
Open Settings Tap the settings icon (gear) in the top-right corner of the Profile screen.
4
Delete Account Scroll down and tap "Delete Account". A confirmation dialog will appear. Confirm to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Option B: Request deletion by email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by sending an email to:

slama.walid1985@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your SportTracking account. We will process your request within 30 days.

3. Data Retained

Some data may be retained for a limited period after account deletion:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Crash logs (Firebase Crashlytics)Up to 90 daysFirebase default retention for debugging
Aggregated analytics data (Firebase Analytics)Up to 14 monthsFirebase default retention (anonymized, not personally identifiable)
Chat reports filed by other usersUp to 90 daysSafety and moderation purposes

Retained data is either anonymized or not personally identifiable. It cannot be used to reconstruct your account or identify you.

4. What Happens After Deletion

  • You will be logged out immediately from the app.
  • You will no longer appear on leaderboards or in event participant lists.
  • Your chat messages in event groups will be removed.
  • Any events you created will be deleted.
  • Your AI Coach conversations and training plans will be deleted.
  • Your referral code will be deactivated.
  • Your saved routes will be removed.
  • You can create a new account with the same email address if you wish to use SportTracking again, but your previous data will not be recovered.
