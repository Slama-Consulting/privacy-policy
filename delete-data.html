Delete Your Data

Last updated: March 13, 2026

SportTracking by SLAMA Consulting allows you to delete specific data without deleting your entire account. This page explains what data you can delete, how to do it, and when data is automatically removed.

1. Delete a Trip (Activity)

You can permanently delete any of your recorded sport activities (trips).

1
Open Trip HistoryTap the History tab in the bottom navigation bar.
2
Select a tripTap on the trip you want to delete to open the Trip Detail screen.
3
Tap the trash iconIn the top-right corner, tap the trash icon (🗑).
4
Confirm deletionA confirmation dialog will appear. Tap "Delete" to permanently remove the trip.

Data deleted: Trip record, all GPS track points, speed, distance, calories, elevation, and weather data associated with the trip. Deleted from both your device and our servers.

2. Delete an Event

Event creators can permanently delete events they have created.

1
Open EventsTap the Events tab in the bottom navigation bar.
2
Select your eventTap on the event you created to open the Event Detail screen.
3
Tap the trash iconIn the top-right corner, tap the trash icon (🗑). This button is only visible if you are the event creator.
4
Confirm deletionA confirmation dialog will appear. Tap "Delete" to permanently remove the event.

Data deleted: Event details (title, description, location, date), participant list, and all associated notifications. Deleted from both your device and our servers.

3. Delete a Chat Message

You can delete your own messages in event group chats.

1
Open the event chatGo to an event, then tap the chat icon in the top bar to enter the group chat.
2
Long-press your messagePress and hold on the message you want to delete. A menu will appear.
3
Tap "Delete"Select the Delete option from the menu.
4
Confirm deletionA confirmation dialog will appear. Tap "Delete" to remove the message.

Data deleted: The message content is marked as deleted and no longer visible to any users in the chat. You can only delete your own messages.

Reporting: To report another user's inappropriate message, long-press their message and select "Report", then choose a reason.

4. Delete Notifications

You can delete individual notifications or clear all notifications at once.

Delete a single notification:

1
Open NotificationsTap the Notifications tab in the bottom navigation bar.
2
Tap the X buttonOn the notification you want to remove, tap the X (close) icon on the card. The notification is deleted immediately.

Delete all notifications:

1
Tap the sweep iconIn the top-right corner of the Notifications screen, tap the sweep (clear all) icon.
2
ConfirmA confirmation dialog will appear. Tap "Delete" to remove all notifications.

Data deleted: Notification records are permanently removed from both your device and our servers.

5. Delete AI Coach Conversations

You can delete your AI Coach conversation history and training plans.

1
Open AI CoachTap the AI Coach tab in the bottom navigation bar.
2
Tap the history iconIn the top bar, tap the history icon to view your conversation list.
3
Select a conversationTap on the conversation you want to delete.
4
Tap the trash iconIn the top-right corner, tap the trash icon (🗑).
5
Confirm deletionA confirmation dialog will appear. Tap "Delete" to permanently remove the conversation.

Data deleted: Conversation messages, AI responses, and training plan suggestions. Deleted from both your device and our servers (Firebase Firestore).

6. Delete Saved Routes

You can remove routes that you have saved from your trip history.

1
Open Trip HistoryTap the History tab in the bottom navigation bar.
2
Go to Saved RoutesTap the Saved Routes section or navigate to your saved routes list.
3
Swipe to deleteSwipe left on a saved route to reveal the delete option, then tap "Delete".

Data deleted: The saved route reference is removed. The original trip data remains unaffected unless you separately delete the trip.

7. Edit Profile Data

You can modify your personal information at any time:

1
Go to Profile → EditTap the Profile tab, then tap the "Edit" button.
2
Modify your dataYou can update your name, gender, date of birth, weight, height, fitness level, and profile photo.
3
SaveTap "Save" to apply changes. Previous values are overwritten.

8. Automatic Data Deletion

Some data is automatically cleaned up by the app:

Data Type Auto-Deleted When What Is Removed
Past eventsAfter the event end time has passedThe event and all its chat messages are permanently deleted from servers (checked every hour)
Event-related notificationsWhen the associated event is deleted or expiresAll notifications linked to the event are removed
Reported user messages (auto-moderation)When a user receives 2+ reports in the same eventAll messages from the reported user in that event are automatically removed
AI Coach conversations older than 6 monthsAfter 6 months of inactivityConversation messages and AI responses are permanently removed from servers

9. Delete Your Entire Account

If you want to delete your account and ALL associated data, please visit our Account Deletion page for instructions.
