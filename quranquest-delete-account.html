Delete Your Account

QuranQuest

Last updated: March 10, 2026

SLAMA Consulting ("we", "our", or "us") respects your right to delete your account and associated data from the QuranQuest mobile application. This page explains how to request account deletion and what data is affected.

⚠ Warning: This action is permanent

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your personal data, Quran memorization progress, reading history, gamification data (XP, badges, streak), leaderboard scores, and game progress. This action cannot be undone.

1 How to Delete Your Account

Option A: Delete from within the app (Recommended)

1
Open QuranQuest Launch the app and make sure you are logged in.
2
Go to your Profile Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar.
3
Open Settings Tap the settings icon (gear) in the top-right corner of the Profile screen.
4
Delete Account Scroll down and tap "Delete my account". You will be asked to type "DELETE" to confirm. You may need to re-authenticate with your password for security. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

Option B: Request deletion by email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by sending an email to:

slama.walid1985@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your QuranQuest account. We will process your request within 30 days.

3 Data Retained

Some data may be retained for a limited period after account deletion:

Data TypeRetention PeriodReason
Crash logs (Firebase Crashlytics)Up to 90 daysFirebase default retention for debugging
Aggregated analytics data (Firebase Analytics)Up to 14 monthsFirebase default retention (anonymized, not personally identifiable)

Retained data is either anonymized or not personally identifiable. It cannot be used to reconstruct your account or identify you.

4 What Happens After Deletion

  • You will be logged out immediately from the app.
  • You will no longer appear on leaderboards.
  • All your memorization progress and review schedules will be lost.
  • Your reading history and khatma count will be removed.
  • All badges, XP, and streak data will be permanently deleted.
  • Your subscription will not be automatically cancelled. Please cancel your subscription through Google Play before deleting your account.
  • You can create a new account with the same email address if you wish to use QuranQuest again, but your previous data will not be recovered.
